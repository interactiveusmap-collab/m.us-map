Welcome

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy document outlines the types of personal information that is received and collected by Interactive USA Map and how it is used.

1. Personal Data

We do not collect any personal information that can identify you directly (such as your name, email address, or phone number) unless you voluntarily provide it to us through direct contact.

2. Log Files

Like many other websites, Interactive USA Map makes use of log files. The information inside includes IP addresses, browser type, ISP, date/time stamp, referring/exit pages, and number of clicks to analyze trends and administer the site. These are not linked to any personally identifiable information.

3. Cookies & Web Beacons

Currently, we do not use first-party or third-party tracking cookies. Our website is static. However, once we begin displaying advertisements through Google AdSense, third-party cookies will be used to serve ads based on prior visits.

4. Google AdSense

Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on visits to this site and others. Users may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.

5. Policy Changes

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. Effective immediately upon posting.

6. Contact Us

If you have questions, please contact us at contact@interactiveusmap.info.
